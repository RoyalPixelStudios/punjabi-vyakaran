Privacy Policy

Punjabi Vyakaran — Last Updated: 29 November 2025

Welcome to Punjabi Vyakaran, an educational app developed and published by Royal Pixel Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how the app collects, uses, and protects information.

1. Introduction

Punjabi Vyakaran is an educational application that helps users learn Punjabi grammar. We are committed to protecting your privacy. The app only collects minimal, non-personal information required for performance, stability, and safety of the app.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email, phone number, or address.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Data (Non-Personal)

To improve app stability and performance, Punjabi Vyakaran uses certain Android and Firebase services. These may automatically collect non-personal diagnostic and usage data, including:

  • App performance metrics (Android Vitals)
  • Crash logs and error reports (Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Device type, OS version, and technical information
  • Anonymous usage information that helps improve app stability

This data does NOT identify you personally.

2.2 Backup & Restore Data

If Android’s system-level Backup & Restore is enabled on your device:

  • Some app data (like saved progress, bookmarks, or settings) may be backed up to your Google account automatically by Android, not by us.
  • We do not have access to your Google backup data.

You can disable backups from your device settings anytime.

3. How We Use the Information

The automatically collected data is used only for:

  • Fixing bugs and app crashes
  • Improving performance and reliability
  • Ensuring smooth user experience
  • Understanding which features need improvement

We do not use the data for advertising, tracking, profiling, or selling to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following trusted third-party services:

4.1 Firebase Crashlytics

Used for crash reporting and diagnostics. Crashlytics may collect:

  • Crash logs
  • Device model
  • OS version
  • App version
  • Non-personal technical information

Crashlytics is provided by Google Firebase. Please refer to Google Firebase's privacy documentation for details.

4.2 Android Vitals (Google Play)

Used to improve app health and performance. Google may collect aggregated, anonymized performance metrics.

4.3 Content Delivery & Web Fetching

Some grammar topics or content may be fetched from online sources. This does not include personal data and does not track user identity.

5. Children’s Privacy

Punjabi Vyakaran is safe for users of all age groups. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults.

6. Data Security

We follow best practices to ensure that:

  • No personal data is collected
  • All diagnostic data is securely handled by Google Firebase services
  • No data is sold or shared with unauthorized parties

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new “Last Updated” date.
