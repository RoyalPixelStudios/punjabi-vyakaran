Privacy Policy

PSTET Exam Preparation — Last Updated: 28 January 2026

Welcome to PSTET Exam Preparation, an educational mobile application developed and published by Royal Pixel Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the app.

1. Introduction

PSTET Exam Preparation is designed to help candidates prepare for Punjab State Teacher Eligibility Test (PSTET) through practice papers, mock tests, and exam-oriented content.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. The app does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, phone number, or physical address.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Non-Personal Data

To ensure smooth functioning and improve user experience, the app may collect limited non-personal information automatically through Android and Google services, including:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version and performance metrics
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data
  • Anonymous usage statistics

This information is used strictly for app improvement and does not identify individual users.

2.2 Test Progress & History

Your test attempts, scores, and progress are stored locally on your device to provide features such as:

  • Attempt history
  • Result analysis
  • Answer review

This data is not uploaded to our servers and remains on your device.

2.3 Backup & Restore

If Android’s system-level Backup & Restore feature is enabled on your device:

  • Some app data may be backed up to your Google account
  • We do not access or control this backup data

3. How We Use Information

Collected data is used only for:

  • Improving app stability and performance
  • Fixing crashes and technical issues
  • Enhancing user experience
  • Ensuring exam features work reliably

We do not sell, trade, or misuse any user data.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Firebase (Crashlytics)

Used for crash reporting and diagnostics. Firebase Crashlytics may collect anonymized technical data such as:

  • Crash reports
  • Device and OS information
  • App usage diagnostics

4.2 Google AdMob (Advertising)

PSTET Exam Preparation displays ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may use the Android Advertising ID to serve and measure ads.

Users can manage or disable personalized ads from their device settings. We do not link advertising identifiers to personal identity.

5. Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for students and exam aspirants. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or any user.

6. Data Security

  • No personal data is stored by the app
  • Local data remains on the user’s device
  • Third-party services follow Google security standards

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.
