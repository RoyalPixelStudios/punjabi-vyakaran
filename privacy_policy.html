Privacy Policy

Punjabi Vyakaran — Last Updated: 30 December 2025

Welcome to Punjabi Vyakaran, an educational app developed and published by Royal Pixel Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how the app collects, uses, and protects information.

1. Introduction

Punjabi Vyakaran is an educational application that helps users learn Punjabi grammar. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Data (Non-Personal)

To improve app stability and performance, Punjabi Vyakaran uses certain Android and Firebase services. These may automatically collect non-personal diagnostic and usage data, including:

  • App performance metrics (Android Vitals)
  • Crash logs and error reports (Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Device model, OS version, and technical information
  • Anonymous usage data to improve app stability

This data does not identify you personally.

2.2 Backup & Restore Data

If Android’s system-level Backup & Restore feature is enabled:

  • Some app data (such as settings or saved progress) may be backed up to your Google account automatically by Android.
  • We do not have access to your backup data.

You can disable backups anytime from your device settings.

3. How We Use the Information

The automatically collected data is used only for:

  • Fixing bugs and crashes
  • Improving performance and reliability
  • Ensuring a smooth user experience
  • Understanding which features need improvement

Advertising in the app is handled by trusted third-party services such as Google AdMob, in accordance with Google Play policies. We do not use data for profiling users or selling personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following trusted third-party services:

4.1 Firebase Crashlytics

Used for crash reporting and diagnostics. Crashlytics may collect:

  • Crash logs
  • Device model
  • OS version
  • App version
  • Non-personal technical information

Crashlytics is provided by Google Firebase. Please refer to Google Firebase’s privacy documentation for details.

4.2 Android Vitals (Google Play)

Used to monitor app quality and performance. Google may collect aggregated, anonymized performance metrics.

4.3 Content Delivery & Web Fetching

Some grammar content may be fetched from online sources. This does not include personal data and does not track user identity.

4.4 Advertising (Google AdMob)

Our app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may use the Android Advertising ID to serve and measure advertisements.

Users can opt out of personalized advertising through their device settings. We do not collect, store, or link the advertising identifier to any personally identifiable information.

5. Children’s Privacy

Punjabi Vyakaran is suitable for users of all age groups. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.

6. Data Security

  • No personal data is collected by the app
  • Diagnostic data is handled securely by Google services
  • No data is sold or shared with unauthorized parties

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.
